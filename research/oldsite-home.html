Home style cakes and bakes made with locally sourced ingredients.

Made with local butter and eggs, all of our cakes are small batch hand made on the premises. Our coffee roasters are based in Penryn and the meat used in our sausage rolls is locally reared . Our suppliers include Kig and Keus, The Cornish Larder Company, Paul's Bakery, Trewithen Dairy, Colin Carter's, Trevaskis Farm butchery and Katjacks.
