Frequently Asked Questions

What are you opening hours?

This time of year we are open Monday to Friday 9.30 to 13.30. As we are take away only, our opening and closing times may alter on days with either heavy rainfall or high winds. If we do plan to open late or close early, our website and social media will have updates.

Do you have indoor seating?

We are take away only. However, we do have a small bench outside and there is seating in front of Barnoon car park adjacent to the Tate Gallery.

Do you use local products?

Our ingredients for our cakes and sandwiches are all sourced from local producers where possible.
